# How to Reach Pelling from NJP, Bagdogra, and Kolkata — 2026 Guide

> **Quick Answer — Key Facts**
>
> - **Nearest airport:** Bagdogra (IXB), ~140 km / 4.5–5 hours by road.
> - **Nearest railway station:** New Jalpaiguri (NJP), ~140 km / 4.5–5 hours by road.
> - **From Kolkata:** Overnight train to NJP (~12 hours), then taxi to Pelling. Direct drive is 700+ km and takes 2 days — not recommended.
> - **From Gangtok:** ~115 km, 4.5 hours; **from Darjeeling:** ~75 km, 3–3.5 hours.
> - **Do Indians need an Inner Line Permit for Pelling?** **No.** According to the Sikkim Tourism Department, ILP is required only for foreign nationals entering Sikkim. Indian citizens carry a photo ID.
> - **Cheapest option from NJP/Bagdogra:** Shared jeep, ₹400–₹700 per seat (2026 rates).

**How to reach Pelling** is one of those questions where the answer depends entirely on where you are starting. Pelling sits in West Sikkim at about 2,150 metres, and there is no airport or railway station in town. You get there via Siliguri, and the choice between **NJP, Bagdogra, or a direct road trip from Kolkata** depends on your budget, your travel dates, and whether you are carrying elderly parents or small children. This guide covers every sensible route with April 2026 fares, honest drive times, and one thing most blogs skip: whether you need an Inner Line Permit.

## Quick Answer — The Decision Tree

- **Flying in?** Fly to **Bagdogra (IXB)**, drive to Pelling. ~140 km / 4.5–5 hours.
- **Taking the train?** Get off at **New Jalpaiguri (NJP)**, drive to Pelling. ~140 km / 4.5–5 hours.
- **Driving from Kolkata?** Overnight train to NJP is still the sanest option, then taxi. Direct drive is 700+ km and two days.
- **Already in Sikkim?** From Gangtok: ~115 km / 4.5 hours. From Darjeeling: ~75 km / 3–3.5 hours.

## Do You Need an Inner Line Permit for Pelling?

**No.** This is the single most common myth about travelling to Pelling. Indian citizens do not need any permit to visit Pelling or most of West Sikkim. The Inner Line Permit (ILP) is required only for **foreign nationals** visiting Sikkim, and additional restricted-area permits are needed for North Sikkim (Lachung, Lachen) and parts of East Sikkim — but not Pelling. Carry a valid photo ID (Aadhaar, voter ID, passport) and you are fine.

## Bagdogra (IXB) to Pelling

Bagdogra Airport, near Siliguri, is the nearest commercial airport to Pelling.

- **Distance:** ~140 km
- **Drive time:** 4.5–5 hours (road conditions vary)
- **Route:** Bagdogra → Sevoke → Teesta Bazaar → Jorethang → Geyzing → Pelling

**Bagdogra to Pelling taxi fare (2026):**
- Shared vehicle (per seat, if available): ₹500–₹700
- Private sedan: ₹5,000–₹6,500 one-way
- Private SUV (Bolero / Innova): ₹6,500–₹8,500 one-way

**Direct flights to Bagdogra from major cities:**
- Kolkata: 1 hour, multiple daily flights from IndiGo, Air India, Vistara (where applicable)
- Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Bengaluru: 1–3 stops

**Honest note on the drive:** The last 40 km from Jorethang upward is winding mountain road. If anyone in your group gets car-sick, take Avomine before setting off and break the journey at Teesta Bazaar for a lunch stop.

## NJP (New Jalpaiguri) to Pelling

The practical choice for most Bengali families from Kolkata, Durgapur, Patna, and Asansol. Overnight train, morning pickup, Pelling by late afternoon.

- **Distance:** ~140 km
- **Drive time:** 4.5–5 hours
- **Route:** NJP → Siliguri bypass → Sevoke → Teesta Bazaar → Jorethang → Geyzing → Pelling

**NJP to Pelling taxi fare (2026):**
- Shared jeep (Sumo/Bolero, per seat): ₹450–₹600 — the backpacker and budget-family favourite
- Private sedan: ₹5,000–₹6,500 one-way
- Private SUV: ₹6,500–₹8,500 one-way

**NJP to Pelling shared jeep — the details:**
Shared jeeps leave from the SNT/Sikkim Jeep Stand near the NJP-Siliguri road. They depart when full, mostly between 8:00 AM and 1:00 PM. Book a full back-row seat if you are travelling with luggage — the rear overhang is tight. Travel time is similar to private taxi but with more stops.

**Tip for Bengali families:** A private taxi for 4–6 people almost always works out cheaper per person than the train + shared jeep combo, once you factor in luggage, comfort, and the certainty of leaving on your schedule. ## Kolkata to Pelling

The Bengali primary route. Three main options:

### Kolkata to Pelling by Train + Road (most popular)
- **Train to NJP:** Darjeeling Mail (12343) / Uttar Banga Express / Padatik Express / Kanchankanya Express / Teesta Torsa Express. Overnight, 10–12 hours.
- **Taxi from NJP to Pelling:** 4.5–5 hours.
- **Total door-to-door time:** ~18 hours.

### Kolkata to Pelling by Flight + Road
- **Flight to Bagdogra:** 1 hour.
- **Taxi from Bagdogra to Pelling:** 4.5–5 hours.
- **Total door-to-door time:** ~8 hours (much quicker, more expensive).

### Kolkata to Pelling by Road
- **Distance:** ~700 km.
- **Drive time:** 16–20 hours of driving; should be split across two days with a halt in Siliguri.
- **When it makes sense:** If you own the car, have the time, and genuinely enjoy driving. Otherwise, train + taxi is smarter.

## Siliguri to Pelling

Siliguri is the transit town 10 km from both NJP and Bagdogra.

- **Distance:** ~135 km
- **Drive time:** 4.5 hours
- **Siliguri to Pelling bus (SNT):** Limited service, usually 7:00 AM departure, around ₹280–₹350 per seat. Government-run, reliable, cheaper than a shared jeep but much slower.
- **Siliguri to Pelling shared jeep:** Same rate tier as NJP — ₹450–₹600 per seat.

## Gangtok to Pelling

If you are on a Gangtok → Pelling circuit (common for first-timers doing North and West Sikkim together).

- **Distance:** ~115 km
- **Drive time:** 4.5 hours
- **Route options:** Via Ravangla (more scenic, slightly longer) or via Singtam (faster, standard).
- **Gangtok to Pelling taxi fare (2026):** ₹5,500–₹7,500 private; ₹500–₹700 per seat shared.

Shared jeeps leave from the Gangtok Jeep Stand near Vajra Stand, mostly in the morning. Afternoon departures are rare.

## Darjeeling to Pelling

- **Distance:** ~75 km
- **Drive time:** 3–3.5 hours (steep descent then climb)
- **Darjeeling to Pelling taxi fare (2026):** ₹4,000–₹5,500 private. Shared jeeps exist but run irregularly — confirm locally.
- **Route:** Darjeeling → Ghoom → Jorethang → Legship → Geyzing → Pelling.

Often the best leg of a Darjeeling-Pelling-Gangtok circuit; very scenic through tea gardens and Teesta-Rangeet valley.

## Pelling's Nearest Railway Station and Airport

- **Nearest railway station:** New Jalpaiguri (NJP), ~140 km.
- **Nearest airport:** Bagdogra (IXB), ~140 km.

Both are close to Siliguri and effectively serve as Pelling's transit hub.

## A Note for Bengali Families Travelling with Kids or Elderly Parents

This is the audience we see most, so some specifics:

- The **Teesta Bazaar** stop (~2.5 hours into the drive from NJP/Bagdogra) is the sensible midway break. Clean toilets, basic restaurants, river view. Budget 30 minutes.
- If someone tends to get car-sick, the winding section starts after **Jorethang** — time the break just before it, not after.
- The last stretch into Pelling is dark after 5:30 PM in winter. **Aim to arrive by 4:00 PM** if your group includes senior citizens or small children.
- Bengali-speaking drivers are common out of NJP — mention this when booking if it would help.

## Pelling Road Conditions (April 2026)

The NH-310 and SH-510 stretches are in reasonable condition post-April 2026. Monsoon months (June through September) occasionally bring landslides that cause detours via Jorethang — add 1–2 hours on those days. Always check with your hotel the day before a monsoon-month arrival.

## How to Reach Pelling FAQ

**What is the NJP to Pelling distance?**
Approximately 140 km, 4.5–5 hours by road depending on traffic and weather.

**Do Indians need a permit for Pelling?**
No. Only foreign nationals need an Inner Line Permit for Sikkim, and even then Pelling is not in a restricted zone.

**What is the cheapest way to reach Pelling from NJP?**
Shared jeep from the SNT Jeep Stand. ₹450–₹600 per seat (April 2026). Departures mostly between 8 AM and 1 PM.

**Which is closer to Pelling — NJP or Bagdogra?**
Both are roughly 140 km away. Bagdogra is the airport (for fliers), NJP is the railway station (for train travellers). They are 10 km apart near Siliguri.

**How do I reach Pelling from Kolkata overnight?**
Take the Darjeeling Mail or another NJP-bound overnight train, then a pre-booked taxi from NJP. Total door-to-door about 18 hours.

**Can I reach Pelling in monsoon?**
Yes, but expect delays. June through September has occasional landslide detours. Don't plan tight connections on the return leg. See our [best time to visit Pelling](/blog/best-time-to-visit-pelling) guide for the month-by-month honesty.

**How far is Gangtok from Pelling?**
About 115 km, 4.5 hours by road. The Ravangla route is scenic, the Singtam route is faster.
