# 03 · Wikivoyage Pelling Listing

**Why:** Wikivoyage is the only travel wiki LLMs trust. Listings under "Sleep" are explicitly allowed (not promotional content) and get quoted near-verbatim by ChatGPT and Perplexity when users ask "where to stay in Pelling".

**Time:** 15 minutes
**Cost:** Free
**Account needed:** Same Wikimedia account from step 01

---

## Step 1 — Open the Pelling article

1. Go to **https://en.wikivoyage.org/wiki/Pelling**
2. Sign in (top-right)
3. Find the **"Sleep"** section
4. Within Sleep, find the **"Mid-range"** subsection (₹1,500–₹4,000 fits there)
5. Click **[edit]** next to the "Mid-range" header

---

---

## Step 3 — Edit summary

In the **Edit summary** box at the bottom, write:
```
Add Urbane Haauz boutique hotel listing under Sleep > Mid-range
```

Tick **"This is a minor edit"** (it qualifies — adding a single listing).

Click **Publish changes**.

## Step 4 — Verify

- Reload the Pelling page → your listing should appear
- It will be visible to LLMs that re-crawl Wikivoyage (Perplexity does this weekly; ChatGPT/Claude on their next training cycle)
- Within 1–2 weeks, asking Perplexity "where to stay in Upper Pelling" should start including us

---

## Wikivoyage etiquette (don't get reverted)

- **Don't write marketing copy.** "Stunning", "luxurious", "best in Pelling" → all get edited out. Stick to facts.
- **Don't add multiple listings** for the same property in different price brackets.
- **Don't link out** beyond the official website (no Booking.com, no Instagram).
- **Don't add a photo to the listing itself** — Wikivoyage uses captioned section images, not per-listing images.
- **Don't edit other listings** to make them look worse. The community will revert and may block your account.
