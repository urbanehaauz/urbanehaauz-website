# Pelling Hotel Market Context

## Market Geography

**Upper Pelling vs Lower Pelling**
- Upper Pelling: Higher elevation, direct Kanchenjunga sightlines, 20–30% price premium
- Lower Pelling: Town center, more budget options, views obstructed by buildings
- **Urbane Haauz is in Upper Pelling — this is its #1 competitive advantage**

## Pricing Strategy

Peak season (Oct–Nov, Feb–Mar):
- Standard: ₹1,800–2,200
- Deluxe: ₹2,500–3,000
- Premium: ₹3,200–3,900
- DORM: ₹600–900/bed

Off-season (Jul–Sep monsoon, Jan):
- Standard: ₹1,200–1,500
- Deluxe: ₹1,700–2,200
- Premium: ₹2,400–3,100
- DORM: ₹400–600/bed

## Primary Audience: Bengali/Bihari Families

- Travel from Kolkata: NJP train + shared cab/taxi (most common), or fly to Bagdogra
- Travel from Patna/Ranchi: Direct cab or train to NJP
- Typical stay: 2–3 nights in Pelling as part of wider Sikkim circuit
- Booking behavior: Heavy OTA users (MakeMyTrip especially) but increasingly direct for better rates
- Decision factors: Mountain view (key), cleanliness, family amenity, meal plan availability
- Peak travel windows: Durga Puja (Oct), winter break (Dec–Jan), Holi break (Mar)

## Secondary Audience: Budget Backpackers

- Age: 20–35, solo or small groups
- Booking: Hostelworld, Booking.com, word of mouth
- Key need: DORM beds, social atmosphere, local info, storage
- Price sensitivity: ₹500–1,000/night absolute ceiling
- Unique about Urbane Haauz: DORM in a boutique property = both comfort AND price

## Key Attractions Near Urbane Haauz

- Pemayangtse Monastery: ~2.3 km (oldest in Sikkim, major draw)
- Rabdentse Ruins: ~3 km (ancient Sikkimese capital)
- Khecheopalri Lake: ~26 km (sacred lake, day trip)
- Pelling Skywalk: ~1 km (glass bridge, tourist draw)
- Singshore Bridge: ~25 km (highest suspension bridge in Asia)
- Yuksom: ~42 km (trekking base for Kanchenjunga)
- NJP Railway Station: ~117 km (~4.5 hour drive)
- Bagdogra Airport: ~130 km (~4.5–5 hour drive)
