**Date**: 2026-04-21

## TL;DR

Phase A fixed the biggest foundation bugs (HashRouter, wrong roomCount, missing OG image). What's left is **content depth + entity signals**: per-page schema is thin, `/rooms` and `/experiences` are effectively orphaned from the navbar, there are zero Room/FAQ/Review nodes in JSON-LD, two NAP items still drift (stale gmail on schema, placeholder WhatsApp in booking outage banner), and `/pelling-2.0` is `noindex` but listed in sitemap (a conflict Google will flag).

**Biggest ROI this week** (P0): wire `/rooms` + `/experiences` into the navbar, remove `/pelling-2.0` from sitemap, fix the placeholder WhatsApp fallback, compress hero-image.png + orchid-flower.png to WebP.
