## 0. Executive Summary — Where We Can Actually Win

The Pelling SEO landscape is thin. The top-10 is crowded with OTA listings (MakeMyTrip, Booking.com, Agoda, Yatra, Goibibo), a handful of travel blogs that rank for everything (Holidify, Tripoto, TravelTriangle), and a few hotel PDPs that chase only branded terms. Almost no one in Pelling writes deep, trip-planning content. That is our opening.

**Three strategic plays:**
1. **Own "Upper Pelling"** — nobody clearly differentiates Upper vs Lower/Middle Pelling in content. A property branded "Upper Pelling" can capture this entire micro-neighborhood cluster with 2-3 well-linked posts.
2. **Own "Kanchenjunga view hotel" with specificity** — most hotels say "mountain view" vaguely. A single page with the balcony angle, best viewing hours, weather, and photos wins.
3. **Be the only DORM + boutique hybrid** — no competitor is targeting "dorm bed Pelling" or "hostel Pelling boutique." This is a completely uncontested cluster.

**What to deprioritize:** Head terms like "hotels in pelling" (OTA-dominated). Chase the long tail and AI-answer-engine (GEO) queries instead.
