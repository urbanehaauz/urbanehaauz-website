## Kanchenjunga Views

### Daytime Clear

### Cloudy/Moody

## Seasonal

### Spring (Mar–May)
> Rhododendron bushes in full bloom (red and pink flowers) with a snow-capped Himalayan peak visible behind them. Bright, saturated colours. A stone pathway leads through the flowers toward a boutique hotel entrance. Spring in Sikkim.

### Monsoon (Jun–Aug)
> A boutique hotel building viewed through a rain-streaked window. Clouds enveloping the surrounding mountains. Warm interior glow from the restaurant visible through the glass. Moody, atmospheric, cozy-despite-the-rain aesthetic.

### Autumn (Sep–Nov)
> Golden-hour light illuminating a mountain valley in autumn. Clear sky, no haze. A boutique hotel terrace with wooden furniture in the foreground, prayer flags strung overhead. Warm amber tones. Post-monsoon clarity.

### Winter (Dec–Feb)
> Snow-dusted railing of a hotel balcony with a dramatic view of snow-covered Himalayan peaks under a crisp blue winter sky. Frost on the metal. A warm blanket draped over a chair. Clear, cold, beautiful.

## Notes for the founder
- Always review the generated image before posting — AI can hallucinate architectural details
- If the mountain looks wrong (wrong shape, wrong snow pattern), regenerate
- Keep a folder of "approved" images to reuse on low-content days
- Real photos from the property ALWAYS outperform AI images — use these prompts only when real photos aren't available
