## Notes
- Always use reviewer's first name
- Echo at least one specific detail from their review
- Sign with a real founder name (Ayan, Shovit, or Souvik)
- Never copy-paste verbatim — adapt 2-3 words minimum per response
- For negative reviews, always offer the WhatsApp direct line
